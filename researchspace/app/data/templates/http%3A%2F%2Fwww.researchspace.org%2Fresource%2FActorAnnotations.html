Semantic Annotation

ResearchSpace provides an intersection between meaningful structured data and analytical narratives.

It allows you to write about individual resources (annotations) or general articles (narratives). These narratives can be underpinned by the data saved in the clipboard and visualised in the narratives themselves to create digital essays backed up with more than just bibliographic citations. These essays are related to the resources that they reference.
